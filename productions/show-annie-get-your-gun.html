Annie Get Your Gun

With lyrics and music by Irving Berlin, Annie Get Your Gun is a fictionalized version of the life of Annie Oakley, a sharpshooter who starred in Buffalo Bill's Wild West, and her romance with sharpshooter Frank Butler. Featuring such classic Musical Theater songs like "Anything You Can Do, I Can Do Better," "You Can't Get a Man With a Gun," and "There's No Business Like Show Business," the summer just got hotter at Stage Door Players. Featuring the sparring lovers from last summer's hit, "Kiss Me, Kate" Paige Mattox and Bryant Smith as Annie and Frank.

July 14 through August 6, 2017
Lyrics and Music by Irving Berlin
