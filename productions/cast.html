Spencer G. Stephens

Spencer G. Stephens is an Atlanta-area performer, puppeteer and educator.  He has performed for many theatres in the metro Atlanta area including Dad's Garage Theatre, Synchronicity Performance Group, Georgia Ensemble Theatre, and the Center for Puppetry Arts where he's been a Resident Puppeteer and director since 2002. He was last seen at Stage Door Players in 2007 in "Home for the Holidays". He is a Suzi Bass Award Winner and a two time Metropolitan Atlanta Theatre (MAT) Award winner. Thanks to Robert for inviting me to play at SDP again. Much Love to my family and friends for all of their encouragement and to Larry for his incredible patience. 

Vallea E. Woodbury

Vallea E. Woodbury is delighted to make her Stage Door Players debut! Atlanta credits include The Crucible at Actor's Express, Simply Simone at Theatrical Outfit, Abigail 1702 and Singles in Agriculture at Aurora Theatre, Tell Me My Dream at Alliance Theatre, and Mama Bear at Out of Box. Regionally, she has appeared with Milwaukee Rep (The Color Purple, The Musical; A Christmas Carol). Drury Lane Oakbrook (Ragtime), The Studio Theatre TDS (Company), and The Second City Chicago, among others. Vallea has an MFA from the University of Georgia, is a Northwestern University graduate and trained at the School at Steppenwolf and The Second City Chicago Improv Conservatory. www.vallea.com 

Melissa "Lola" Youngblood

Melissa "Lola" Youngblood is so excited to reprise the role of "Nell" at the Stage Door Players after 15 years! She is a native of Miami, FL where she cultivated her talent, passion and drive for the Performing Arts. Lola has been performing in regional and International theatres for more than a decade in such productions as Ain’t Misbehavin’, Smokey Joe’s Café, and two productions at The Alliance Theatre, Jelly’s Last Jam and Twist, An American Musical directed by Mrs. Debbie Allen! She is now settled in Atlanta where she teaches Theatre in Atlanta Public Schools, is a Shuler Award Adjudicator for the High School Musical Theatre Awards as well as a performer on film and television. You may have seen Lola in TV Films such as "Bessie" for HBO Films and "Crazy, Sexy, Cool... The TLC Story" for VH1. She has also done numerous commercials and voiceovers for popular video games. Lola is the lead singer of a Jazz Combo called "The Melody Masters featuring Lola" who perform at events all over the Metro Atlanta area. Lola considers herself highly blessed and favored to be able to have enjoyed a successful performance career on stage/tv/film and continues to strive for the top with the support of her family, especially her Mama watching over her from Heaven!
