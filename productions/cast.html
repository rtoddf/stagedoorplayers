Ben Davis

Benjamin is thrilled to make his Stage Door Players debut! Ben was last seen as Gilley in I'm Not Rappaport (Aurora Theatre) and Anthony in Sweeney Todd (Actor's Express). He has also appeared at the Alliance Theatre, Horizon Theatre, Aris Theatre, Synchronicity, Theatrical Outfit, and Dad's Garage. Film & TV credits: The Fat Boy Chronicles, Fun World, The D.U.F.F., and "Dream Writers." Ben is the Founder and Executive Artistic Director of the Atlanta Musical Theatre Festival, coming this summer. He is a proud graduate of UGA and the Aurora Apprentice Company! Up next, you can see Ben as Bobby Strong in Urinetown at the Warehouse Theatre in Greenville, SC. www.BenjaminTaylorDavis.com

Jessica De Maria

Jessica De Maria is so glad to spend her second summer with her Stage Door Family. Jessica is an Atlanta based performer and writer. She also serves as the Development Manager of the Atlanta Musical Theatre Festival.  Stage Door Players: THE MYSTERY OF EDWIN DROOD. Regional: THE LIGHT IN THE PIAZZA (Theatrical Outfit), SWEENEY TODD, MURDER BALLAD (Actor's Express), DAMN YANKEES, EDGES (Atlanta Lyric Theatre), LES MISERABLES, MARY POPPINS, PETITE ROUGE: A CAJUN RED RIDING HOOD (Aurora Theatre), THE GRIMM LIVES of the INBETWEENS (Alliance Theatre). Off-Broadway: THE LAST TIME WE WERE HERE (The Laurie Beechman Theatre, as part of the New York Musical Theatre Festival). Jessica is also the co-writer of WHAT'S PAST, with her writing partner Chase Peacock, which will be featured at the Atlanta Musical Theatre Festival this summer as well as in the THRESHOLD New Play Festival at Actor's Express in December. Jessica also co-wrote the folk-rock musical recently workshopped at Synchronicity Theatre, THE LAST TIME WE WERE HERE. Upcoming you can check her out in as Lucy in THE THREEPENNY OPERA at 7 Stages. See more at www.jessicademaria.com
