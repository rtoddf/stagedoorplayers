The Cast of SECRETS OF A SOCCER MOM

ADENA BRUMER

ADENA BRUMER* is overjoyed to be in her first production at Stage Door Players!  Currently, you can see her as a not-so-nice "Check-in Lady" in the YouTubeRed series Champaign ILL.  She was most recently seen in The Bikinis at Art Station, I Can't Techno with Uncle Bernie Productions, and played an angry Irish pirate vampire in The Anointing of Dracula: A Grand Guignol at Theatre Emory.  Adena sings with Wild Iris Rose (an acoustic duo) and Rockin' Train (a classic rock/modern rock cover band). She is also currently on the podcast, It Came From The Bottom, which reviews the worst 100 movies on IMDb - she doesn't recommend ANY of them.  Before moving to her new home of Atlanta, Adena lived in the Twin Cities.  While there, she sang with the acappella quartet, Swing Set, and Tony Ortiz and his Guns of Soul. She also performed at The Guthrie, The Ordway Center for Performing Arts, The History Theatre,  MN Jewish Theatre's, Theatre de la Jeune Lune, Chanhassen Dinner Theatres, Mixed Blood, Ten Thousand Things, Theatre Latte Da, Park Square, and she continues to perform in a live radio version of It's a Wonderful Life each December at The St. Paul Hotel.  Adena is a proud member of Actors Equity Association and SAG-AFTRA.  www.adenabrumer.com

BRITTANI MINNIEWEATHER

BRITTANI MINNIEWEATHER is excited to make her Stage Door Players Debut! She is a graduate of Wofford College where she received a BA in Theatre. Her original musical, Sundays at 4, recently premiered at The Atlanta Musical Theatre Festival this past August.  Credits: Freaky Friday and Madeline's Christmas (Horizon Theatre), Little Shop of Horrors (Actor's Express), Sweat (The Warehouse Theatre), Sordid Lives (Out Front Theatre), Polkadots (The Legacy Theatre), 9 to 5: The Musical (Onstage Atlanta), For Colored Girls… and The Laramie Project (IA Playhouse), Legally Blonde (Charleston Stage), and Debbie Allen's Brothers of the Knight (Ms. Debbie Allen). Brittani would like to thank her hubby, mommie, and granny in heaven. You can see her next in Jump at Actor's Express. Follow her: @britminnieweather

HANNAH MORRIS

HANNAH MORRIS is thrilled to be joining this team in her first show at Stage Door Players! Hannah is a graduate of Point Park Conservatory of Performing Arts in Pittsburgh, PA (B.F.A. 2015). She is also an alumnus of the Horizon Theatre Acting Apprentice Company (2016-2017). She has been seen onstage in productions around Atlanta at Out of Box Theatre, OnStage Atlanta, and Act3 Productions, as well as the Atlanta AppCo Alumni Series. Many thanks to Suehyla and Robert for the opportunity, and much love to friends and family for their unwavering support.
