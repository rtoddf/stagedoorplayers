The Cast of Death by Design

Chase Steven Anderson

Chase Steven Anderson is thrilled to be making his Stage Door Players debut! Recent Stage Credits include Of Mice & Men and Charlotte's Web (Serenbe Playhouse), and Love/Sick (Aurora). Film: Tyler Perry's Too Close to Home (TLC), Nashville (ABC), Powers (PlayStation), Satisfaction (USA), Ride Along (Universal), Necessary Roughness (USA), One Tree Hill (CW), plus commercials for Cheez-It & Farm Rich. Look for him in the upcoming Warner Brother's comedy, Bastards, coming soon to Theaters. Chase lives in Atlanta and is proudly represented by Houghton Talent, Inc. EDU: BFA Acting/Directing from Columbus State University. Love to family and friends, and many thanks to Robert & the creative team for this opportunity.

Daniel Burns

Daniel Burns is thrilled to be returning to Stage Door Players. Previously he appeared on this stage as Frank in Godspell and as Bazzard in The Mystery of Edwin Drood. Other recent roles include Don Price in Big Fish (Theatrical Outfit), Peter in Company and Fogg in Sweeney Todd (Actors Express), Enoch Snow in Carousel and Padre in Man of La Mancha (Serenbe Playhouse). He would like to thank his family, friends, and Paige for their continuous love and support.

Kelly Criss

Kelly Criss is so happy to be back at SDP! She has been working as a professional actor (Don't Dress for Dinner, Moon Over Buffalo, and Miracle on South Division Street all here at Stage Door), producer, and director for several years in the Atlanta area. Other favorite Atlanta credits include Hamlet and Romeo & Juliet (Atlanta Shakespeare Co.), 4000 Miles (Aurora Theatre), and Desdemona (Weird Sisters Theatre Project). For the past four years, she served as Artistic & Education Manager at Horizon Theatre. Recently, she has joined the High Museum of Art as Manager of Special Projects. She is thrilled to work with this wonderful cast and production team. Love to Matt!

Joanna Daniel

Joanna Daniel is delighted to be returning to SDP to play in this farcical mystery!! During the past couple of years she has been seen at Arís in The Artist Man and the Mother Woman, and Georgia Ensemble Theatre in Calendar Girls and Charley’s Aunt. She also played the role of Lord Capulet in Fern Theatre’s all-female production of Romeo and Juliet, as well as the title character in their all-female production of King Lear. When not performing, Joanna keeps busy behind the scenes at Arís (pronounced Ah-Reesh), Atlanta’s Celtic Theatre Company, where she plays the roles of Treasurer, Producer and Founding Member. Many thanks to Robert and Suehyla and the whole team at SDP, and most of all to you for supporting live theatre!

Kevin Stillwell

This is Kevin's first appearance at Stage Door, and what a delightful show to debut in! Kevin is an actor, director, fight choreographer and voice over artist. Recent credits include: Appropriate (Actor's Express), Three Penny Opera & The Breakers (7 Stages), Calendar Girls & R&J (G.E.T.), Lasso of Truth (Synchronicity), Confirmation HBO, among others. Thanks to Suehyla, and to Melich...FLY.

Bryn Striepe

Bryn Striepe is really trying hard not to start this bio off with how happy she is to be a part of her first Stage Door production, because everyone says that. The problem is, it’s true. Atlanta credits include Horizon: Freed Spirits (MJ); Synchronicity: In the Next Room, or the vibrator play (Mrs. Givings), Lasso of Truth (The Amazon); Actor’s Express: Seminar (Izzy); Out of Box: Rapture, Blister, Burn (Avery); The Weird Sisters: Hot Pink (Taatanya); Pinch’N’Ouch: Wall Street Wedding (Becky); The Fern Theatre: Romeo & Juliet (Romeo), Being Alive (Jen), Macbeth (Lady Macbeth). Originally from California, she holds a BA in Theatre from Willamette University and is a previous Actor’s Express intern. Thank you, just generally.

Pat Young

Pat Young is thrilled to be doing his first show at Stage Door Players! Pat is an actor, improviser, and entertainer with a BA in theatre from FSU. His last shows include The Tribute Artist with Process Theatre, Knock Em' Dead with Gypsy Theatre, and Lillian Likes It with Essential Theatre. Pat owns a trivia company called Trivial Matters, which has the best trivia games in Atlanta, and that isn't an exaggeration. He also has a Guinness World Record to his name. You should ask him about it. Pat would like to thank his lady friend and the cats. Though lets be honest, the cats probably don't care about any of this.

Sarah Newby Halicks

Sarah Newby Halicks is delighted to return to Stage Door this year! Previous SDP credits include "I Do, I Do, I Do." Other Atlanta credits include "Two Gentlemen of Verona" and "Much Ado about Nothing" (Shakespeare Tavern), "The Addams Family" (Onstage Atlanta), and you also may have seen her staggering around in a Shitfaced Shakespeare production of "A Midsummer Night's Dream." Many thanks to Robert and Suehyla for their vision and guidance. Love to family and to Sir Matthew. Represented by Aligned Stars Agency.
