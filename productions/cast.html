The Cast of THE OUTSIDER

Kelly Criss

Kelly Criss is delighted to be back at Stage Door Players. Previous credits include Death by Design, Miracle on South Division Street, Moon Over Buffalo, and Don't Dress for Dinner. Atlanta theatre credits include the Alliance Theatre, the Atlanta Shakespeare Company, Aurora Theatre, Essential Theatre, Georgia Ensemble Theatre, Serenbe Playhouse, Synchronicity, Theatre Buford, Theatre in the Square, Theatrical Outfit, and 7 Stages. She is the Director of Corporate & Individual Giving at Aurora Theatre, and also a founding member of the Weird Sisters Theatre Project, currently serving on its board of directors. She sends love to family and friends.

Mark Gray

Mark Gray is honored to be returning to Stage Door Players. Mark was last at SDP in Laughter On The 23rd Floor. You might remember Mark from other SDP productions, such as the Suzi Award winning Let Nothing You Dismay, or Mass Appeal, It’s A Wonderful Life A Radio Play, Lend Me A Tenor and Blithe Spirit. Additional theatre credits include 1:23 for Synchronicity Theatre as well as Based on a Totally True Story, Bent, Gypsy, Bee Luther Hatchee, Doll Plays and Auntie Mame all for Actors Express. Marks television credits include Showtime’s Comedy Club Network, Days of Our Lives on NBC and One West Waikiki for ABC. Mark has also been featured in the films World Traveler, Bobby Jones - Stroke of Genius and Tyler Perry’s Meet The Browns. Mark sends thanks to his fellow cast members, who make up this lunatic asylum. It is always fun to have your friends join in the fun. A big thank you to our director, Matthew for his insight and wisdom. Words are not enough to express the many thanks to Robert for all that he has done for SDP and bringing quality theatre to Dunwoody. Saving the biggest thanks for Virgia, his wife for more than 35 years for agreeing to be a theatre widow – once again.

Sarah Newby Halicks

Sarah Newby Halicks is delighted to return to Stage Door Players! As a graduate of UGA and the Shakespeare Tavern apprenticeship program, previous SDP credits include I Do, I Do, I Do and Death by Design. Other theatre creditss include Actor’s Express, the Shakespeare Tavern, and the Alliance Theatre. Much love to Robert and Matt for the opportunity to come back and play! Represented by BMG and RSH Management.

Garrett McPherson

Garrett McPherson is excited to be making his debut with Stage Door Players and would like to thank the wonderful cast and crew who have made this show possible. Previous Atlanta credits include Simon in 20th Century Blues (Live Arts Theatre), Jack in Girls Life (Pinch N’ Ouch Theatre) and Hal in Picnic (Merely Players Presents). Garrett holds a BFA in Theatre Performance from Baylor University. He would like to thank his wife, Meredith, for her unending support, encouragement and love.

John Markowski

John Markowski is thrilled to be returning to Stage Door Players! Some of John's favorite SDP credits include Max in Lend Me a Tenor, Robert in Boeing, Boeing, Clown in The 39 Steps, and Eric Sheridan in Unnecessary Farce. He has been proud to also perform at Georgia Ensemble Theatre, Aurora Theatre, Atlanta Lyric Theatre, Onstage Atlanta, and The Process Theatre Company. By day, John is a full-time actor/educator and teaching artist with Kaiser Permanente. He would like to thank Robert, Matthew, the amazing SDP production team, and his family and friends for their constant love and support.

Doyle Reynolds

Doyle Reynolds is very excited to be back home at Stage Door where he’s appeared in Laughter on the 23rd Floor, Dial M for Murder, Let Nothing You Dismay and Sylvia. Other Atlanta credits include: The Importance of Being Earnest (Theatre Buford); Night Must Fall (Georgia Ensemble); The Book of Will (Theatrical Outfit); Nell Gwynn, Anne Boleyn, The Vibrator Play (Synchronicity); 6 Degrees of Separation, 100 Saints You Should Know, Finn In the Underworld, Some Men, I Am My Own Wife (Actor’s Express); Bob (Geva Theatre and Aurora Theatre); Mr. Universe, The Violet Hour (7 Stages). Other credits include shows with Dad’s Garage Theatre, Jewish Theatre of the South, Theatre in the Square, Pinch ‘n Ouch, and many others.  Thank you for supporting live theatre!

Parris Sarter

Parris Sarter is absolutely excited to be making her Stage Door Players debut with such a fun show and cast! She holds a M.A. in Performing Arts from Savannah College of Arts and Design. Her credits throughout the Atlanta area include: The Revolutionists (7Stages), Eclipsed (Synchronicity Theatre), Angels in America and An Octoroon (Actor's Express), The Cake (Horizon Theatre), and The Grown-Up (Out of Box Theatre). She is a three time Suzi Bass nominee and a two time winner for Best Ensemble for both Eclipsed and Angels in America. Thank you for supporting live theater! Ok, let's have a good time!
