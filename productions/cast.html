The Cast of SMOKEY JOE’S CAFÉ

Xylina Cassandra

Xylina Cassandra is elated to perform again at Stage Door Players! You may have her in Kiss Me, Kate! two seasons ago. She has also appeared on "Green Leaf" on the Own Network as a singer. Xylina is the 2018 co-host for the holiday season Christmas on Ice at MGM’s Beau Rivage Resort & Casino. Recent credits include: The Buddy Holly Story (Georgia Ensemble), The Color Purple (West Coast Black Theatre), Memphis (Aurora Theatre/Theatrical Outfit), BeeHive, Dreamgirls (Atlanta Lyric) and countless others. In addition to her stage career, Xylina is the creator and founder of Artist 360 Kids where she teaches life skills through the arts to thousands of students every year. www.artist360kids.com - "Mom, This one is for you. I never would have made it, with you. Thank you." #mylife

Fennear Eady

Fenner Eaddy, a native of Norfolk Virginia, began his training at The Governor’s School for the Performing Arts. Upon his completion of high school, Fenner was accepted into Morehouse College where he received his BA in Drama. Previous shows include White Christmas, Smokey Joe's Cafe, 42nd Street (Mill Mountain Theatre), In the Heights, Memphis (Aurora Theatre and Theatrical Outfit), My Fair Lady, Dirty Rotten Scoundrels, Jesus Christ Superstar, Damn Yankees, Catch Me if You Can, Chicago, Cats, Sophisticated Ladies, Anything Goes, The Producers, 42nd Street (Atlanta Lyric Theatre), Carmen, Porgy and Bess (Atlanta Opera), Memphis, Fly (Theatrical Outfit), and Godspell (Legacy Theatre).

Trey Getz

Trey Getz is excited to be returning to the Stage Door Players stage in Smokey Joe's Café, his first show back in Atlanta after 5 years! Past SDP credits include: Jester (Once Upon a Mattress) and Stanley (High Society). Favorite credits include: Phil Davis (White Christmas), Mark (Rent) and Anthony (Sweeney Todd). After singing 4 contracts at Tokyo Disneyland with the show "Minnie Oh! Minnie", Trey will be returning to Japan to debut a brand new Christmas show at Tokyo Disney Sea this coming winter season! Thank you to Robert, Nick and all the SDP family for having me back! Love to all my family and friends!!!

Solita Parrish

Solita Parrish has performed in musical reviews for Stone Mountain Park and Six Flags for over 10 years. She has also been in productions around Atlanta such as Dreamgirls, Sister Act (starring as Deloris), and many performances with the New Depot Players in Conyers, Ga. Although this is Solita's first production with Stage Door Players, she is excited to see what the future holds! 

Kiona D. Reese

Kiona D. Reese is grateful to be making her debut with Stage Door Players! After earning her BA in Theatre Arts from Birmingham-Southern College, she had the pleasure to be a member of the 2016-17 Actor's Express Intern Company where she performed in Little Shop of Horrors and The Crucible (Suzi-winning Best Play 2017). Other Atlanta credits include Dinosaur! (Alliance Theatre) and The Rocky Horror Show (Out Front Theatre Company). She'd like to thank her parents for their constant love and support, as well as teaching her how to stretch a greenback dollar bill from here to kingdom come. For updates, follow her on Instagram at @lovealwayskdr.

Lyndsay Ricketson

Lyndsay Ricketson is thrilled to be back at Stage Door Players this summer in Smokey Joe's Café! Other regional credits include: Kiss Me Kate (Lois Lane), A Chorus Line (Maggie), Next to Normal(Natalie), Shrek: The Musical (Teen Fiona/Peter Pan), Ragtime (Evelyn Nesbit), Pump Boys and Dinettes (Rhetta Cupp), Sweeney Todd (Johanna), Little Women (Beth) and Hairspray in Concert (Penny). Special thanks to my family and friends for putting up with my crazy schedule. To my husband, Lowrey, you are my favorite! Discover your noblest dreams within the cathedral of your heart.

George P. Roberts

George P. Roberts is ecstatic to be performing at home this summer! Favorite Regional: The Color Purple (Harpo), Carowinds WinterFest, Hairspray (Seaweed). Elon University – BFA Music Theatre. Infinite love and gratitude to GOD, Mom & Dad, Akilah, Samira, grandparents, and extended family and friends. To Grandma and Pop Pop: this summer is for you! "Dream, Believe, Live!"

Kendrick Taj Stephens

Kendrick Taj Stephens, a Newnan, GA native, is thrilled to join such a talented cast in his first production with Stage Door Players! Some of his most recent stage productions include A Solid Gold Summer Music Revue (The Historic Earl Smith Strand Theater), the voice of Audrey ll in a Marietta production of Little Shop of Horrors and Schroeder in You're a Goof Man, Charlie Brown (Northside UMC). Taj originated the role of Albert Brooks in the Newnan Historical Society's inaugural production of Flies at the Well and was featured in the Georgia premiere of Priscilla, Queen of the Desert and The Rocky Horror Show, both produced by the award-winning Out Front Theater Company. A revered entertainer throughout the metro-Atlanta area, he has been a featured performer at the Cobb Energy Center, has worked with Screen Gem and Tyler Perry Studios and has directed behind the scenes of the electronic music festival Tomorrow World. When Taj is not on stage or in front of the camera he is an image consultant and Visual Merchandiser for H&M, having opened several stores in the Southeast. Taj would love to thank his family, friends, co-workers and especially his other half, Joe Arnotti for the continuous support! @kendricktaj

Brian Turner

Brian Turner is absolutely thrilled to be part of Smokey Joe's Cafe! This is his first show with Stage Door Players, and he is so very grateful for this opportunity! Recent theatre credits include Damn Yankees and Peter Pan with the Atlanta Lyric Theatre, and The Who's Tommy with Springer Opera House. Brian is an alumnus of both Ithaca College and The Boston Conservatory's BFA Musical Theatre program. He would like to thank Almighty God, his family, friends, the cast and the crew of Smokey Joe's for all their love and support!
