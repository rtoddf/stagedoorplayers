The Cast of Living on Love

George Deavours

George Deavours is excited to be returning to Stage Door Players in Living on Love, with such an amazing cast! Recent credits include: Alfred Doolittle in My Fair Lady, Sitting Bull in Annie Get Your Gun, Maurice in Beauty and the Beast, John Adams in1776,Murray the Cop in The Odd Couple; Gus/ Bustopher Jones in CATS, Amos Hart in Chicago, Lt. Brannigan in Guys and Dolls, Ezekiel Foster in White Christmas, Seth Lord in High Society; and Cigar in Gypsy. Other favorite roles include Beadle Bamford in Sweeney Todd, Zaza in la Cage aux Folles, Major-General Stanley in The Pirates of Penzance, and Melvin P. Thorpe in The Best Little Whorehouse in Texas. A wig designer for over 260 shows, he is also professional hairstylist, and has a studio at French Door Salon in Duluth. In July he will be appearing as Edna Turnblad in Hairspray at the Legacy Theatre.

Michael Strauss

Michael Strauss is very excited to be making his Stage Door Players debut & to be working with Robert Egizio for more than 2 hours. Last seen on the Atlanta stage as the somewhat less crazy Italian, Signor Naccarelli in Theatrical Outfit’s The Light In The Piazza, he’s also worked at Actor’s Express, G.E.T., ArtStation, and Theater Emory. Some of his favorite roles include Pontius Pilate in a long ago gospel production of Jesus Christ Superstar in Philadelphia, Sherlock Holmes at the Arts Center Of Coastal Carolina and his recent 70s Trilogy, working with Barry Williams, Joyce DeWitt & Barbara Eden in 3 shows at The Alhambra in Jacksonville, FL. Special thanks to Marianne Fraulo for her continued generosity with her Italian expertise, to Robert for having me on board, to Valory for continuing to be the amazing love of my life & to you, the audience, for supporting live theatre. Grazie!
