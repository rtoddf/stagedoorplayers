Meet the cast of Out of Order

Stephanie Wilkinson (Pamela)

Stephanie was most recently seen as Baroness Elsa Schraeder in the Atlanta Lyric Theatre's production of Sound of Music. Other Atlanta credits include: Steel Magnolias (Clairee), A Little Night Music (Madame Armdeldt), The Secret Garden (Rose), Red Velvet Cake War (Elsa), South Pacific (Nellie), and Man of LaMancha (Aldonza).

She holds degrees in music and has been vocal coach and music director for productions at the GA Governors Honors Program, Atlanta Lyric Kids, and various programs around Atlanta.
