Stage Door Players Directions

Stage Door Players is located at 5339 Chamblee Dunwoody Road approx one block South of Mt Vernon Rd and Chamblee Dunwoody Road in the North Dekalb Cultural Arts Center.

Coming from West

  • Take I-285 East to Exit 29 - Ashford Dunwoody Rd
  • Turn Left onto Ashford Dunwoody and continue past Perimeter Mall
  • Turn Right at Ashford Center Parkway and continue to the next light
  • Turn Left onto Chamblee Dunwoody Road
  • Take the 2nd driveway on your Right into the North Dekalb Cultural Center

Coming from East

  • Take I-285 West to Exit 29Ashford Dunwoody Rd
  • Turn Right onto Ashford Dunwoody and continue past Perimeter Mall
  • Take a Right at Ashford Center Parkway and continue to the next light. Turn Left onto Chamblee Dunwoody Road
  • Take the 2nd driveway on your Right into the North Dekalb Cultural Center

Coming from South

  • Head North on 400 to Exit 5a (Dunwoody)
  • Merge onto Abernathy Rd and turn left at Mt Vernon Rd
  • Continue a few miles to Chamblee Dunwoody Road and turn right
  • We're on the left in the North Dekalb Cultural Center

Coming from North

  • Head South on 400 to Exit 6 (Northridge Rd)
  • Turn Right off the exit (back over Rt 400) and take a right at the 4-way stop sign onto Roberts Drive
  • This road changes names to Chamblee Dunwoody Road. Continue a few miles
  • We're a block past Mt Vernon Rd on the Left in the North Dekalb Cultural Center

Sylvia Cast

Amanda Cucher

Amanda Cucher's tail is wagging wildly to be back at Stage Door Players since her previous appearances including Madge Geisel in The Game's Afoot, or Holmes for the Holidays, Dana in Beached Wails, Randi in Greetings, and Traci in Spreading it Around. Other local theatrical jaunts include Sister James in Doubt at Out of Box Theater, Evelyn in Evelyn in Purgatory with Essential Theatre Company and Barbara Grant in Perfect Arrangement. She would like to thank Shelly and Robert, dog-friendly beaches, freshly mown grass, the makers of Beggin' Strips, and all of the hands that have fed, patted and supported her along the way over the years.

Sylvia Cast

Doyle Reynolds

Doyle Reynolds is very happy to be at appearing at Stage Door for the first time and working again with Shelly McCook. He is an Associate Artist with Actor’s Express where he has performed in "6 Degrees of Separation," "100 Saints You Should Know," "Finn In The Underworld," "Some Men," "I Am My Own Wife" (Suzi Bass Award) and "Rescue and Recovery." At Geva Theatre and Aurora Theatre: "Bob." At Synchronicity: "The Vibrator Play." At 7 Stages: "Mr. Universe" and "The Violet Hour." At Theatre In The Square: "Dirty Blonde," "Sylvia," "Gross Indecency, The 3 Trials of Oscar Wilde," and "Red Scare On Sunset." Other theatre credits include work with Dad’s Garage Theatre, Pinch ‘n Ouch, Jewish Theatre of The South, Horizon Theatre Company, Soul-stice Repertory Theatre, barking dog theatre and others. Thank you for supporting live theatre!
