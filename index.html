I Love You, You're Perfect, Now Change

March 22 through April 14, 2013
Friday and Saturday @ 8:00pm, Sunday Matinees @ 2:30pm

This musical celebration of the mating game takes on the truths and myths behind that enigma known as "the relationship." Exploring the journey from dating and waiting to love and marriage, to the agonies and triumphs of in-laws and newborns, this hilarious revue pays tribute to those, young and old, who have loved and lost, and have dared to ask, "Say, what are you doing Saturday night?"

Book and Lyrics by Joe DiPietro
Music by Jimmy Roberts

Tickets can also be purchased online from: TIX.com

Meet the Cast of I Love You, You're Perfect, Now Change

Erin Meadows

Erin is so happy to return to Stage Door since last appearing in A Grand Night For Singing. Favorite credits include: Glinda the Good Witch in The Wizard of Oz; Chester the Cat in Bunnicula; Sister Mary Leo in Nunsense; Lois Lane and Bianca in Kiss Me, Kate; Roberta in Zanna, Don't; and Peggy the Pin-Up Girl in The Andrews Brothers. Erin is a proud graduate of Woodward Academy and the Conservatory of Theatre Arts at Webster University.

Meet the Cast of I Love You, You're Perfect, Now Change

Craig Waldrip

Craig returns to Stage Door Players after previously appearing in A Christmas Survival Guide, Pippin, And The World Goes 'Round and Home For the Holidays. Craig has appeared on many local stages, including Alliance Theatre, Actor's Express, Aurora Theatre, Fabrefaction Theatre, Habima Theatre, Dad's Garage and many others. Some favorite local credits include Molina in Kiss of the Spider Woman, John Hinckley in Assassins, Curly in Oklahoma!, The Emcee in Cabaret, Frank N Furter in The Rocky Horror Show, Hedwig in Hedwig and the Angry Inch and Janitor/Reporter in See What I Wanna See.

Theater Classes For Children

ONCE UPON A BALLET comes to STAGE DOOR PLAYERS.

Once Upon a Ballet & Musical Theater™ offers unique dance and performing arts programs for children ages 2-14. From our Creative division to our Upper school classes, our mission is to foster the creative spirit in your child, by providing sound technical training in a nurturing environment. Handmade costumes, delicate props and timeless stories are an essential part of our Creative curriculum, while Classic ballets and Broadway musicals are the focus of our Upper school curriculum.

What is your child's passion? Ballet! Tap! Acting! Singing! Once Upon a Ballet & Musical Theater™ has it all. Contact Once Upon a Ballet & Musical Theater™ at onceuponaballetatl@gmail.com and ask about special Introductory Offers for Stage Door Players patrons!!
