Boeing, Boeing

Parisian lothario Bernard has three fiancées; American, Italian and German, all three airline hostesses. Usually able to keep them all straight with their various layovers, he finds himself in need of assistance from his put upon friend Robert when a last minute scheduling change brings all three to the apartment at the same time in this swinging 60’s French farce by the same author who brought us "Don't Dress for Dinner."

Meet the cast of Boeing, Boeing
