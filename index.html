Sylvia

A modern romantic comedy about love, marriage, and a talking dog. Greg brings home a dog he found in the park, bearing only the name "Sylvia" on her name tag. A street-smart mixture of Lab and Poodle, Sylvia becomes a major bone of contention between husband and wife. The marriage is put in serious jeopardy until, after a series of hilarious and touching complications, Greg and Kate learn to compromise, and Sylvia becomes a valued part of their lives.

May 15 through June 7, 2015
By A.R. Gurney

Sylvia Cast

James Baskin

James Baskin is delighted to return to Stage Door Players, where he first appeared in Out of Order. James has performed with several Atlanta-area theaters, including Georgia Ensemble Theatre, Theater Emory, Onstage Atlanta, and Essential Theatre. He is grateful beyond measure to Shelly for the chance to tell this story and for the encouragement and inspiration she has provided in the past. Thanks also to Robert and the SDP team (and to you who read this) for making live theater a reality in this place. Finally, for expert coaching on how to converse with a dog, a special thank you to Chancellor.

Sylvia Cast

Jo Howarth

Jo Howarth is delighted to be back at Stage Door Players where she was honored to receive the Woodie Award for her performance as Mrs. Baker in Butterflies are Free. Other favorite roles in Georgia include works by award winning playwright Topher Payne, such as Swell Party (Georgia Ensemble Theatre), Evelyn in Purgatory (Essential Theatre), Lakebottom Proper (Springer Opera House), & Don’t Look at the Fat Lady (Process Theatre – Best Actress/Metropolitan Atlanta Theater), as well as, Suddenly Last Summer (Actor’s Express), Driving Miss Daisy (Theatre Gael), Doubt (Out of Box), and Johnny Drago's Trash (Process Theatre). Jo is an affliated artist of NYC based Flying Carpet Theater. Many thanks to this wonderful cast & crew and to you, for supporting live theater.
