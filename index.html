AIN'T MISBEHAVIN'

The inimitable Thomas "Fats" Waller rose to international fame during the Golden Age of the Cotton Club and that jumpin' new beat, swing music. With familiar songs like Honeysuckle Rose, This Joint is Jumpin’, I Can’t Give You Anything But Love, and the title tune, Ain't Misbehavin' evokes the delightful humor and infectious energy of this American original as a versatile cast struts, strums and sings the songs that he made famous in a career that ranged from uptown clubs to downtown Tin Pan Alley to Hollywood and concert stages in the U.S., Canada and Europe.

May 24 through June 16, 2019
Music and Lyrics by Thomas "Fats" Waller
