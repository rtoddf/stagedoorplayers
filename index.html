Godspell 2012

Based on the Gospel According to St. Matthew, and featuring a sparkling rock score by Stephen Schwartz, GODSPELL 2012, incorporates the changes and updated references used in the 2012 Broadway Production, and boasts a string of well-loved songs. As the cast performs the international hit, "Day By Day" and many others, the parables of Jesus Christ come humanly and hearteningly to life.

  • May 16 through June 8, 2014
  • Regular performances Thursday, Friday and Saturday evenings, Sunday afternoons at 2:30
  • Preview performance May 15, 2014

Music and New Lyrics by Stephen Schwartz

Directed by Brian Clowdus

Musical Direction by Nick Silvestri

Choreography by Bubba Carr

Godspell Cast

Tierra Porter

Tierra Porter is a newcomer to the professional Atlanta theatre community, Tierra is very excited to be apart of this production. Her educational background ranges from Cobb Centre for Excellence in the Performing Arts to Coastal Carolina University where she trained for some time as a BFA Musical Theatre major. She has been on a number of stages in the Metropolitan area. (Strand Theatre, Jennie T. Anderson, Cobb Energy Center, Act 3 ) Last but certainly not least, Tierra would like to thank Brian and Robert for giving her the opportunity of a lifetime!

Godspell Cast

Robert Owenby

ROBERT MITCHEL OWENBY is beyond excited to be making his debut at the Stage Door with this fantastic production of Godspell! Most recently Robert appeared on stage at the Alliance Theatre as Lord Farquaad when he understudied that role and the title role in their production of Shrek. Some of his other favorite credits include Henry Ford in Ragtime (Atlanta Lyric Theatre), Dennis Sanders in Sanders Family Christmas (OnStage Atlanta), and Traveling Man U/S in Songs To Grow On (Alliance Theatre)

Godspell Cast

Courtney Godwin

Courtney is so excited to be returning for her second show with Stage Door Players!  Most recently, you may have seen Courtney in The Legacy Theatre’s remount of The Andrews Sisters, A Christmas of Swing.  She has also appeared in such Atlanta favorites as The Aurora Theatre's Christmas Canteen and as Denise Sanders in The Atlanta Lyric Theatre's remount of Theatre in the Square's long running hit, Smoke on the Mountain.
