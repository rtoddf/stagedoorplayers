On Golden Pond

Ethel and Norman Thayer are spending their summer at their home on a lake called Golden Pond. This year they are visited by daughter Chelsea with her fiancé and his son in tow. Generations humorously and poignantly clash, as the play explores the often turbulent relationships of growing up, and the difficulties faced by a couple in the twilight years of a long marriage.

  • January 24 through February 16, 2014
  • Regular performances Friday and Saturday evenings at 8:00, Sunday afternoons at 2:30
  • Preview performance January 23, 2014

By Ernest Thompson

Directed by Kelly Criss

Theater Classes For Children

ONCE UPON A BALLET comes to STAGE DOOR PLAYERS.

Once Upon a Ballet & Musical Theater™ offers unique dance and performing arts programs for children ages 2-14. From our Creative division to our Upper school classes, our mission is to foster the creative spirit in your child, by providing sound technical training in a nurturing environment. Handmade costumes, delicate props and timeless stories are an essential part of our Creative curriculum, while Classic ballets and Broadway musicals are the focus of our Upper school curriculum.

What is your child's passion? Ballet! Tap! Acting! Singing! Once Upon a Ballet & Musical Theater™ has it all. Contact Once Upon a Ballet & Musical Theater™ at onceuponaballetatl@gmail.com and ask about special Introductory Offers for Stage Door Players patrons!!

On Golden Pond Cast

Theo Harness (Norman Thayer, Jr.) is appearing for the second time at Stagedoor; in Visiting Mr. Green he won a Woody for Best Performance by a Lead Actor (Play) and a Suzi nomination in the same category. Other credits: in JibJam’s Faith Healer, a Suzi nomination for Featured Actor; at Atlanta Lyric Theatre he was Arvide Abernathy in Guys and Dolls, and Herr Schultz in Cabaret with Jackie Prucha; at Art Station: Scrooge in A Broadway Christmas Carol; at Portland Center Stage: Craig Lucas’s This Thing of Darkness, Itimar Moses’ Outrage and Chekhov’s The Seagull; Heartbreak House at the Alliance; James Tyrone in Long Day’s Journey Into Night at Soulstice Rep; and at Actor’s Express: Jeffrey (Sterling Farrell), Chris Coleman’s Oklahoma, Twelfth Night (Malvolio), The Cherry Orchard (Gaev), Cloud 9 (Betty) and so many others.

On Golden Pond Cast

Dori Garziano (Chelsea Thayer Wayne) Leeman holds an MFA in Theatre Performance and a MS in Clinical Counseling Psychology. Currently, Dori teaches Theatre as an Adjunct Professor at KSU and counsels in Forsyth County. Dori has been blessed to work at many theatres throughout metro-Atlanta. She has a passion for using the creative arts as a therapeutic tool with adolescents and young adults for mental health and well-being. Most of all, Dori loves her husband Michael, her dog Jack, and her family.
