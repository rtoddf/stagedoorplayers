Kathy Ellsworth

Props Designer in Residence

From Bark Bars to Rodin’s The Thinker, there’s nothing Kathy Ellsworth can’t come up with for a show! A long time collector of both knicks and knacks, not to mention do-lollies and thingamajigs, she can provide virtually anything a script or a director calls for. Need a whatchamacallit? She’ll have three different kinds to choose from: two of them borrowed for free, and the third bought on sale with a coupon! She’s on a first name basis with every Goodwill, Thrift Store and Flea Market around and she never met a piece of junk that didn’t have some redeeming qualities!
