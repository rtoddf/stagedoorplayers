Charles Welcome

Set Designer/Scenic Artist in Residence

Chuck began his illustrious career at Stage Door Players in January 2003, as the Set Designer for Agatha Christies, The Mousetrap, and has been here ever since!

Chuck has a background in art, interior design and marketing, and has worked in theaters all over the Atlanta Metro, with nearly 100 set designs under his belt!

By day he works as Regional Director for Upscale Showplaces Inc., staging, showcasing and managing vacant homes for sale in the Atlanta Metro.
